Free and ad-free, Home Library allows you to store your personal library and to perform a quick search within it.

My Library allows you to :
- Add a book to your library
- Edit a book in your library
- Remove a book from your library
- Look for a book in your library
- Sort your library by titles, names, genres, ...
- Export your library within an .lb file
- Import a library from a previously exported library

You are free to add official covers for your books.
